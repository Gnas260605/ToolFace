# Skill: Copyright, Originality and Platform Compliance

## Use when

- Adding sources
- Designing prompts/templates
- Publishing content
- Preparing monetization claims
- Writing policies and onboarding

## Mandatory rules

- Attribution does not equal permission.
- Prefer official feeds/APIs.
- Do not reproduce full articles.
- No paywall bypass.
- Keep source and licensing notes.
- Provide takedown process.
- Add meaningful editorial value.
- Detect near-copy.
- Clearly distinguish rumor and confirmed information.
- Human review for high-risk content.
- Do not promise Facebook monetization.
- State that customers remain responsible for publication rights and factual review.
- Maintain privacy, terms, data deletion, and AI disclosure pages.

## Required product warnings

- Minimally altered content may have reduced reach.
- Unoriginal content may be ineligible for monetization.
- AI output can be wrong.
- Source attribution does not automatically grant republication rights.
